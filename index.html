About IEEE

IEEE (pronounced "I triple E") is the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers. We are a group dedicated to professionalism, connections, and our projects.

Our mission

We provide engineers with technical, social, and professional development in the engineering field. We aim to be the leading engineering organization on campus, providing more opportunities for student improvement outside the classroom. We are committed in providing engineers with career enhancement, engineering social events, and growth in their respective professions.
